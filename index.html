Key Sales Agent Functionality

Intelligent Product Knowledge

Connect your Shopify product catalog, your website, and any other sources. These are the exact same sources you use for customer support so you don't need to maintain information in two places. Sales Agent then automatically indexes this information and uses it to answer product questions and make recommendations, no training or writing of automations needed.

Sales Agent has access to more than just product data, though. It can use past orders, customer memories, and prior conversations to personalize answers and recommendations for your customers.

Generating Revenue from Customer Interactions

Encourage your customers to engage with you and your products, creating revenue generating opportunities with every conversation. Siena's AI-generated product questions, which we call Conversation Starters, will prompt your customers to start a conversation by surfacing common product questions. This lowers the friction to starting a conversation. From here, Sales Agent will expertly answer their questions. But customers are not limited to these Conversation Starters. They can ask any product questions they have.

Increase Discoverability for More Conversations

Deploy the Sales Agent across multiple touchpoints with our Web Widgets framework, offering various chat discoverability types and chat interfaces:

  • Embedded Product Search: Embedded UI with product-focused prompts that you can embed anywhere on your site product pages
  • Chat Button: Traditional corner button that launches a chat interface
  • Floating Search Bar: A search bar that "floats" at the bottom of your site and launches the chat window

You can mix and match these discoverability options to suit your needs and provide the right experience for your customers anywhere on your site.

Rich Product Cards With Add to Cart

Deliver engaging product cards with images, descriptions, and pricing in Siena's native Live Chat. This includes direct "Add to Cart" functionality for Shopify so that customers can start their purchase directly within their conversation.

Unified Customer Journey

The Siena Sales Agent bridges pre-purchase and post-purchase experiences, creating a seamless customer journey. You can use the live chat widget as a standalone sales and product experience, but all of Siena's support capabilities can also be delivered through the same interface if you choose to enable those automations. For example, you can automatically processes returns or exchanges while identifying new sales opportunities or recommendations for a replacement. Additionally, Sales Agent recommendations can be delivered through any channel you support today, be it email or SMS in addition to live chat.
